Who we are

A diversified enterprise with a unified vision

Moolya India Private Limited was incorporated on 15 January 2024 under the Companies Act, 2013, and is registered in West Bengal with ROC-Kolkata. From inception we have operated with complete transparency, regulatory compliance and ethical practices.

Today we operate through specialised divisions, each managed by dedicated professionals who bring focused expertise to their domain — from devotional products to digital marketing and public-sector tenders.
